Risks & Mitigating Factors

Risk : Unexpected costs or delays in permitting.

Mitigation : Partnering with top-tier EPC, financial advisor and milestone-based payments.

Risk : Counterparty fails to meet obligations.

Mitigation : Credit screening and long-term contracted PPAs with investment-grade buyers.

Risk : Production variance due to weather.

Mitigation : Diversified geographies and conservative production modeling.

Risk : FX volatility affecting returns.

Mitigation : Hedging strategies and USD-pegged contracts where possible.
